Scene 03 — The Retina
The
Retina.
A thin sheet of light-sensitive tissue
lining the back of the eye.

This is where images form.

Scene 04 — Blood Supply
Blood
Vessels.
A dense network of arteries and veins
radiates from the optic disc,
supplying oxygen to every photoreceptor.

The retina is entirely dependent
on this fragile vascular network.
⚠ Diabetic Retinopathy
The vessels
begin to fail.
High blood sugar weakens capillary walls.
They swell into microaneurysms.
They rupture. They bleed.
The retina starves of oxygen.
Scene 06 — Early Detection
Preventable.
If detected in time.
Diabetic retinopathy is the leading cause
of preventable blindness in working-age adults.

We are building a system to detect it —
from a single retinal photograph.

Act 07 — Architecture

NetB3.

Transfer learning: the backbone already knows
edges, textures, shapes —

The loss descends.
